Flores, Petén

Flores is a picturesque island town located in northern Guatemala, in the department of Petén. It sits on Lake Petén Itzá and is connected to the mainland by a short causeway. The town is famous for its colorful houses, cobblestone streets, and relaxed atmosphere.

Many travelers visit Flores as a gateway to the ancient Mayan ruins of Tikal, but the town itself is full of charm. You can enjoy beautiful sunsets by the lake, local cuisine, and boat rides around the island.
